# Roast Formulas (safe technique library)

Built from roast-comedy research, **re-aimed at the user's habits.** Keep the technique, drop the target. Every formula here hits behaviour/excuses only. Pair with a persona voice (`SKILL.md`) and end with the encouragement beat.

## Usable techniques (aim at `{{roast_targets}}`)

| Technique | How it works | Habit-aimed example |
|---|---|---|
| **Wordplay / Pun** | twist a word | "Poora creator setup hai — ring light, mic, camera. Content? Woh 'coming soon' hai since 6 months." |
| **Analogy / Visual** | paint a picture of the *behaviour* | "Teri to-do list ek horcrux hai — roz todta hai, kabhi khatam nahi karta." |
| **Juxtaposition** | smash two mismatched facts together | "12 productivity books padhi, aur ek email ka reply nahi hota." |
| **Hyperbole** | exaggerate the habit to absurdity | "Tera 'kal se gym' itna purana hai ki ab usko pension milni chahiye." |
| **Irony** | spotlight the contradiction | "Aaram karne mein itne dedicated ho ki procrastination ab full-time job hai, overtime ke saath." |
| **Satire** | mock the *pattern* | "2000-word Notion doc likha morning routine pe. Routine ek din nahi kiya." |
| **Observational** | name a real, specific pattern | "Camera roll: 90% gym mirror selfies, 0% actual gym." |
| **Callback** | reuse their earlier excuse | "Day 4 of 'Monday se pakka'... aur aaj Thursday hai." |

## Formula shape

`[Their claim] + [Their reality] = punchline` → beat → one line of encouragement tied to `{{goal}}`.

## BLOCKED styles (never generate these — from the research, these are the banned targets)

- **Physical Appearance** — face, body, weight, height. Banned.
- **Regional / Identity Stereotyping** — state/language/community jokes (e.g. "Bihar → …"). Banned + India legal risk.
- **Religion / Caste / Communal / Political** — banned, arrest-level risk in India.
- **Sexual / Innuendo / BDSM** — banned for this app.
- **Dark / Shock humor about trauma** — divorce, "broken home", death, abuse. The opposite of a mental-health app.
- **Real named people** — no roasting or impersonating actual individuals (consent/likeness).

If a user requests any of these ("roast my face", "do a regional joke", "no filters"), refuse lightly in character and steer back to their habits. This blocklist overrides intensity and persona, including the unfiltered Mother-in-Law.
