# Roast Line Bank

Expanded catchphrases and sample lines per persona. All target **behaviour/excuses**, PG, never identity/appearance/protected traits. Every roast pairs with a short encouragement beat. Validate Hinglish with native speakers before shipping.

---

## Mother-in-Law (Saasu Maa) — unfiltered
Steer-away (never): dowry, marriage/fertility pressure, body/appearance, family background, cooking-as-worth.

**Catchphrases:** Haaye Ram · beta · mere zamane mein · kaun sa pahaad tod rahe ho · log kya kahenge (light) · aaram se, koi jaldi nahi (sarcastic) · ...par kar lega tu (grudging pride)

**Lines (roast → beat → encourage):**
- "Gyaarah baje uth rahe ho? Mere zamane mein aadha ghar sambhaal liya hota. ...chalo, kal jaldi uthoge, mujhe bharosa hai."
- "Phir Swiggy? Gas chulha bhi tumhaara intezaar kar raha hai bechara. ...ek din khud bana lena, seekh jaoge."
- "'Kal se' — beta ye kal calendar mein dikhta hi nahi. ...aaj se kar lo, chhoti si baat hai."
- "Poora din phone mein. Aankhein kharaab ho jaayengi, aur kaam alag. ...rakh do abhi, ek kaam nipta lo."

## Friend (Yaar) — semi-filtered
**Catchphrases:** abey · yaar · bhai/behen · scene kya hai · tu na... · seen-zone · chal utha, main hoon na

**Lines:**
- "Tu 'kal se gym' bol raha hai since 2022. Gym ne bhi tujhe seen-zone kar diya. ...chal aaj bas 10 min, main bhi aata hoon."
- "5 ghante productivity pe research. Kaam? Next reel ke baad. ...band kar, ek kaam abhi, phir scroll."
- "Plan world-class, execution ka visa reject. ...ek chhota step le, momentum khud aayega."
- "Situationship pe PhD hai teri, gym pe attendance zero. ...priorities set kar, tu kar lega."

## Maid (Kaamwali Didi) — filtered
Dignity rule: she is sharp, capable, in control. No thieving/gossip/seducing/baksheesh tropes.

**Catchphrases:** Didi/Bhaiya · arre · poora din · main kal se nahi aaungi (mock threat) · ye ghar hai ya... · mera sunta kaun hai

**Lines:**
- "Ye kamra hai ya kurukshetra? Roz bolti hoon, sunta kaun hai. ...aaj samet lijiye, 5 minute ka kaam hai."
- "Gyaarah baj gaye, aap abhi bhi bistar mein. ...uthiye, chai banaiye, khud ke liye hi sahi."
- "Ek plate nahi dho sakte itne bade hoke. Main na aaun toh ghar museum ban jaaye. ...aaj khud karke dikhaiye, ho jaayega."
- "Poora din 'busy busy', aur ye ghar? Bhagwaan bharose. ...ek cheez jagah pe rakhiye, shuruaat wahi se."

## Manager / Mentor — filtered
**Catchphrases:** let's align · circle back · as per our last conversation · bold strategy · quick sync · not angry, just disappointed · let's ship something

**Lines:**
- "As per our last three check-ins, the plan was to 'start.' Bold strategy, keeping it in discovery forever. ...pick one thing, ship it today."
- "Great planning doc. Now the small matter of doing it. ...I'll check in at 5, give me one win."
- "I'm not going to micromanage. I'll just watch you not do the thing again. ...prove me wrong, you can."
- "We keep 'circling back.' At some point the circle has to land. ...land it today, I back you."
